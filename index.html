About Me

I am a current PhD candidate at Monash University. My research interests lie in cortical prosthesis with my thesis specifically focussing on bionic vision. I am working to improve the achievable resolution with targeted stimulation in cortical implants through the application of current steering. My other interests relate to the processing of visual motion and how to recreate this using neurobionics. Outside of my thesis work, I teach undergraduate students in both the physiology and engineering faculties at Monash.
